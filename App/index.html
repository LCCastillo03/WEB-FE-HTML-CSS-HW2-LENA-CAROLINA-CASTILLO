"The book is a garden that can be carried in a pocket, a spaceship traveling in a backpack, a weapon to face the best battles and confront the worst enemies, a seed of freedom, a handkerchief for tears. The book is a soft bed and a bed of nails, the book forces you to think, to smile, to cry, to get angry at injustice and applaud the revenge of the just. The book is food, shelter, a seat, clothing that wraps me, a mouth that kisses my mouth. A place that contains the universe."

Benito Taibo
